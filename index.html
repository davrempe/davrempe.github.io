Davis Rempe - About Me

I'm a first year PhD student in the computer science department at Stanford University. My research interests include computer graphics, physically-based simulation, computer vision, and machine learning.


I'm currently rotating with Ron Fedkiw during the fall quarter.

I did my undergrad at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln where I finished with a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics along with a minor in Physics. I also took many classes in the film school dealing with digital graphics, visual effects, and animation.

Outside of school I enjoy traveling whenever possible (I have blogged about most of my international travel), watching soccer, playing music (mostly the bass), and of course movies.
