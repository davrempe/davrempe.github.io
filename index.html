Davis Rempe

I'm a third year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Stanford University advised by Leonidas Guibas. I'm generally interested in computer vision and graphics, machine learning, and physical simulation. I particularly enjoy problems in the area of physical understanding of objects, scenes, and humans.


I've previously interned at Adobe Research and Snap Research. During my first year rotations, I was also able to work with Ron Fedkiw and Doug James.

I did my undergrad at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln where I received a B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics along with a minor in Physics. I also took many classes in the film school dealing with digital graphics, visual effects, and animation.

Outside of school and work I enjoy traveling whenever possible (I have blogged about most of my international travel), watching soccer, playing music (mostly the bass), and movies.
