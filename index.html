Davis Rempe

I'm a 4th year PhD student in Computer Science at Stanford advised by Leonidas Guibas. I'm generally interested in 3D computer vision and machine learning, particularly in understanding dynamic and physical objects, humans, and scenes.


I have interned at Adobe Research and Snap Research and during first year rotations I was able to work with Ron Fedkiw and Doug James.

I enjoy traveling, watching soccer, playing music (mostly the bass), and movies.
