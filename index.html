Davis Rempe

I am a research scientist at NVIDIA in the Toronto AI Lab. I am generally interested in 3D computer vision, graphics, and machine learning, particularly in synthesizing and perceiving dynamic humans, objects, and scenes.


I received my PhD in Computer Science from Stanford University in 2023, advised by Leonidas Guibas. I am grateful to have been supported by the NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship for 2022-2023. Throughout my PhD, I interned at NVIDIA, Adobe, and Snap. I did my undergraduate work at the University of Nebraska in Computer Science and Mathematics along with a minor in Physics.

I enjoy traveling and playing music (mostly the bass).
